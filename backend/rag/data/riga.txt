Riga, the captivating capital of Latvia and the largest city in the Baltic states, offers a delightful blend of medieval charm and vibrant modern culture. The best time to visit this historic gem is during the summer months from June to August, when the weather is warm and outdoor cafes come alive, though December is also magical for its festive Christmas markets. While exploring, your top attractions should include the enchanting Old Town, the iconic House of the Blackheads, and the breathtaking Art Nouveau architecture along Alberta Street. Food lovers should indulge in traditional local food like hearty grey peas with bacon, dark rye bread, and delicious piragi pastries, which can all be sampled at the bustling Riga Central Market. Getting around the city is incredibly easy and convenient for travelers. Riga's historic center is highly compact and best explored on foot, while an affordable and efficient network of trams, buses, and trolleybuses connects you to outer neighborhoods. For longer distances, reliable ride-hailing apps like Bolt offer a quick and inexpensive way to travel, making Riga a stress-free and unforgettable destination to discover.
